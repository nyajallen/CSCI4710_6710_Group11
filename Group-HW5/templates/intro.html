Introduction

This past year has been a whirlwind of uncertainty.
Many of us made to be stuck in our homes with little information on the world outside our doors.
There are many people out there who may share the same outlook as you on the entire situation, but you may not know it.
Well, now you will know with our web application.
We offer a database full of people all over the world that shows you the demographics as well as an interactive map
of people living in the same situation as you.
